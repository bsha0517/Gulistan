Our Story

Rooted in Community, Built for Generations

Named in honour of Sir Syed Ahmed Khan — the great reformer and educationist — our society was founded on the principle that every family deserves a dignified home in a thriving neighbourhood.

We operate as a registered cooperative under the Punjab Cooperative Housing Societies Act, ensuring transparency, democratic governance, and member-first values in everything we do.

  • Democratic governance — every member has a voice
  • Transparent accounts, audited annually
  • Plot allotments by fair ballot process
  • Dedicated parks, mosque, and community hall

"Building homes, nurturing neighbourhood."
